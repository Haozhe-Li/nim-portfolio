## **Reshaping the Order of Cognition at the Edge of the Unknown: The Birth of Omni Knows Neo**

In an era where information surges like a tide only to slip away like fine sand, do we still possess that pure spark known as "curiosity"?

Today, we are doing more than just updating a tool. We are polishing a mirror—one capable of reflecting both the order of the past and the fantasies of the future. **Omni Knows Neo** arrives with a new soul, stepping toward every lone explorer.

## A Visual Psalm: Retro-Futurism and Cold Philosophy

<Video
src="https://cdn.haozheli.com/omni-flowers.mp4"
alt="Flowers - Omni Neo"
caption="Flowers - Omni Neo"

We have redefined the visual language of Neo. This is not merely a UI reconstruction; it is a narrative of "exploration":

* **Retro-Futurism**: We have fused the romantic visions of the last century with modern, high-end industrial design. Shimmering **Cyan** light dances amidst deep shadows, reminiscent of vacuum tubes flickering in a midnight laboratory.
* **The Warmth of Newsprint**: In this digital realm, we have intentionally preserved the grain and texture of physical media. Every interaction feels like flipping through a vintage newspaper delivered from the future.
* **Nordic Neutrality & Restraint**: The overall aesthetic follows a minimalist Nordic philosophy, stripping away the redundant to leave the stage for the content itself. Within this vast, uncharted interface, you are the lone navigator; Neo is your sole North Star.

## The Depth of Wisdom: Global Memory and an Adaptive Soul

<Video
  src="https://cdn.haozheli.com/omni-cube.mp4"
  alt="Cube - Omni Neo"
  caption="Cube - Omni Neo"
/>

Neo has become more "like you"—or rather, it has become an extension of your own cognition.

* **Global Memory**: Neo no longer lives only in the "now." With the ability to recall across separate conversations, it captures your fragmented preferences and deep-seated thoughts. Over time, it evolves into a highly personalized knowledge base unique to you.
* **Intelligent Skills Architecture**: Utilizing a "Progressive Disclosure" framework, Neo balances lightning-fast response times with logical depth. It no longer rushes to flaunt every capability, but instead precisely releases skills based on the context of your needs.
* **The Art of Planning**: Neo now possesses greater initiative. It can deconstruct complex tasks and draft precise To-Do lists. It is no longer a mere Q&A machine, but a strategist fighting by your side.
* **Versatile File Integration**: Neo accepts a broader range of file types—including images, PDFs, Word, Excel, and Markdown—allowing for direct interaction and editing within the Canvas.

# Introducing Omni Pages: Turn Your Deep Research into Public Content

Omni Pages is a powerful new feature that allows you to transform your **Canvas (Deep Research)** reports into beautifully formatted, shareable blog posts. Whether you're a researcher, student, or curious mind, you can now share your AI-powered insights with the world in a single click.

---

## 🚀 How to Publish Your First Page

Publishing your research is seamless. Follow these three steps to go from a query to a published URL:

1.  **Generate Your Research:** Visit [omniknows.xyz](https://omniknows.xyz) and enter any topic. Ensure you select the **Canvas** mode to generate a comprehensive, deep-dive report.
2.  **One-Click Publish:** Once your report is ready, navigate to the **top-right corner** of the Canvas interface and select **"Publish to Pages."**
3.  **Share Your Insights:** A unique, public URL will be generated instantly. You can share this link directly with friends, colleagues, or on social media. Additionally, high-quality reports may be featured on the **Pages Homepage** for the entire community to discover.

> 💡 **Total Control:** You retain 100% ownership of your content. If you want to take a page offline, simply click **"Stop Sharing"** at any time to revert the report back to private.
